Guidance

When to Enable It

Turn these on when you want stronger control over submitted receipts and proof files.

  • Useful for stricter treasury review processes and higher-risk financial workflows.
  • Best used when you want every submitted transaction to include supporting proof.
  • OCR remains available for data extraction and works independently from fraud detection.
  • Leave a toggle off if your team prefers faster entry with manual validation instead.
  • Enable offering protection when you want batch and individual offering uploads screened before review.
