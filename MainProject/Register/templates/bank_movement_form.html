How this works
  • Direct to Bank (Income)

    Records income that goes straight into the bank. No physical cash movement is involved.

  • Paid from Bank (Expense)

    Records an expense that is settled directly from the bank account.

  • Review first

    Nothing is finalized immediately. The form saves a draft and sends you to the review screen.

Entry tips
Use clear reference numbers, upload readable proof files, and add short notes when the transaction needs extra context. This makes review and audit much easier later.
